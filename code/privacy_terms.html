Privacy Policy

Last updated:

Thank you for using Task Manager. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our application.

Information We Collect

Task Manager is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect and store only the following information:

  • Task Data: The tasks you create, including titles, descriptions, due dates, priorities, and status
  • Usage Data: Time tracking information and completion statistics
  • Preferences: Your theme preference (light/dark mode) and sorting preferences

How We Store Your Information

All your data is stored locally on your device using your browser's local storage. We do not:

  • Transfer your data to external servers
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Collect personal identification information

Data Security

Since your data remains on your device, its security depends on your device's security measures. We recommend:

  • Keeping your browser updated
  • Using device security features (passcodes, biometrics)
  • Regularly exporting your data if needed

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the application
  • Modification: You can edit or delete tasks at any time
  • Export: You can export your data using the export feature
  • Deletion: Clearing your browser data will permanently delete all application data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

Important: This application runs entirely in your browser. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.
